# EquipType.cs

This is an enum of all the types of equipment that exist. An equipment type is defined as a type or layer of texture that is drawn on the player (for example, body and shield and wings).

## Values

- Head
- Body
- Legs
- HandsOn
- HandsOff
- Back
- Front
- Shoes
- Waist
- Wings
- Shield
- Neck
- Face
- Balloon
